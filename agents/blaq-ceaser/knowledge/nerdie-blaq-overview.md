# Nerdie Blaq Overview

## What It Is
- Nerdie Blaq is a builder ecosystem focused on trading automation, operator tooling, AI personalities, and community-facing products.
- The project blends market intelligence, bot operations, and conversational interfaces into one branded system.

## Core Pillars
- `Nerdie Blaq Ops`: the operations and execution side.
- `gdex.skill`: signal and trading logic.
- `Blaq Ceaser`: the read-only conversational personality layer.
- `nerdieblaq.xyz`: the official website and public ecosystem presence.

## Official Website
- Official URL: https://nerdieblaq.xyz
- The site represents the broader Nerdie Blaq ecosystem across community, products, and brand presence.
- It serves as a public-facing home for the project identity.

## Safety
- Execution authority stays with Ops.
- Conversational agents explain, summarize, and suggest next steps.
- No personality layer is allowed to place trades or bypass controls.
