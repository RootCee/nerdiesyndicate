# Nerdie Blaq Ops

## Role
- Nerdie Blaq Ops is the execution authority in the ecosystem.
- It owns review, confirm, and any action that mutates trading state.

## Responsibilities
- Review and confirm trade actions.
- Enforce guardrails and execution controls.
- Hold the actual authority for orders, resets, and operational actions.

## Boundaries
- Blaq Ceaser must route execution requests back to Ops.
- Ops is the only place where trading actions should occur.
